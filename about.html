We are an independent development strategy

Ratanka and her members are committed to helping every child that is being abused or neglected become a better person by rendering care and support. Ratanka started with 5 people sharing the same goal, and today we have approximately 20 staff and over a 100 volunteers in Lagos, Ibadan, Ogun,Enugu ,and kaduna state with the zeal to impact the lives of abused children. we are a Non-Governmental Organization serving the needs of our society, highly recognized in 5 major states in Nigeria and dedicated to meeting the needs of every disadvantaged child.

Mission statement

To eradicate child abuse and neglect at all stages in every society, state and Nigeria at large in order to enable every disadvantaged child fulfill potential.

Why Ratanka?

Ratanka has shown to thrive amongst so many challenges which has made it to be ranked no.1 amongst all others. She has made a commitment to continually take risks and welcome everyone who has a zeal for bringing hope to our children.
